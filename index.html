Rupee Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four rupees below. By clicking on a rupee you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You will win the game by matching your number of points to the random number given to you at the beginning of the game. You will lose the game if your points go over the amount of the random number given.

The amount of points each rupee is worth is hidden from you until you click on it.

The value of the random number and the rupees will game each time the game restarts.

Good luck!
